**Commands**

```ini
[summonerlist]       [list|list_add|list_del]

[blacklist]          [blacklist|black_add|black_del]

[summoner_info]      [eloeu|suminfo|mastery]

[misc]               [ching|free|new]

[help]               [help]
```
**Summoner list**
```ini
[b.list:]              [return a summoner list, usage: "b.list"
                        will return your list, "b.list
                        @someone" will return his list]

[b.list_add:]          [add a summoner name to your list,
                            usage: "b.list_add summoner_name"]

    [b.list_del:]          [remove a summoner name to your list,
                            usage: "b.list_del summoner_name"]```
